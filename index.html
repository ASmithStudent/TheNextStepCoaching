About Me

Mario DiPreta

I am a graduate student studying Exercise Physiology at Ithaca College. My job is to help you reach your goals. From losing weight, to gaining muscle, or looking and feeling your best, I am here to guide you through lifestyle changes that will leave you confident, and happy in your life.

​

As a health and fitness enthusiast, my passion is to help others take control of their fitness, health and wellbeing. I coach all populations (youth, high-school, collegiate, and recreational athletes). I am also trained to work with various clinical populations (diabetics, heart disease, and Parkinson's etc).

​

I understand and continue to experience the challenges of personal change, and can help guide you through the confusion brought on by the overwhelming fitness marketing industry. All my programs are based on science and personal experiences. Are you ready?
